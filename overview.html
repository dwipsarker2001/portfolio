Bijoyee Wordpad

Bijoyee Wordpad is a user-friendly word processing app specifically designed for practicing Bijoy Bayanno typing on mobile devices. With this app, you can connect a keyboard using an OTG cable and type directly in Bangla.

The app features an easily accessible sidebar with guidelines for typing using the Bijoy method, helping you quickly master Bangla typing. If you face any issues with OTG connections, you'll also find easy-to-follow tutorials within the app for assistance.

Bijoyee Wordpad is the perfect solution for practicing Bijoy typing on your mobile device.
